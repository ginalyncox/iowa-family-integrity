Founder Bio (Short)

Gina Lyn Cox is a citizen advocate from Altoona, Iowa. She founded the Iowa Family Integrity and Patient Protection Initiative to close legal gaps that punish lawful medical-cannabis patients in family and child-welfare settings. Her work focuses on evidence-based policy, patient rights, and family integrity.
