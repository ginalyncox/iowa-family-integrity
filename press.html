Press Overview

The Initiative aligns Iowa’s medical-cannabis framework with child-welfare practice so lawful patients are never penalized for responsible treatment.

Quick Facts

Name
Iowa Family Integrity and Patient Protection Initiative
Founder
Gina Lyn Cox — Altoona, Iowa
Core Goal
Safeguard lawful medical-cannabis patients from discrimination in family-law contexts.
Standard
Use impairment—not THC presence—as the test of safety.
Scope
Legislative amendments • Iowa HHS guidance • Judicial education
