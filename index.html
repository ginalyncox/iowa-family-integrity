Purpose & Scope

This site centralizes a pragmatic, patient-first framework for Iowa. The initiative aims to strengthen family integrity, set clear impairment standards, curb prohibited practices, and codify perinatal and postnatal safeguards—paired with Iowa HHS guidance, judicial education, and a realistic implementation timeline.

Plain-English Summary

  • Clear definitions reduce ambiguity and prevent misuse.
  • Impairment standard ties state action to demonstrable, present risk.
  • Prohibited practices rein in overreach and protect due process.
  • Perinatal/postnatal safeguards protect patients, infants, and families.
  • Guidance + Bench Memo align agencies and courts for consistent practice.
