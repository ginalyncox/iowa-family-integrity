The Initiative

The Iowa Family Integrity and Patient Protection Initiative promotes patient safety, family stability, and respect for state sovereignty. We advocate for policies that protect registered medical-cannabis patients from being penalized in custody, foster, or adoption cases.

Legislative Reform

The Family Integrity and Medical Cannabis Patient Protection Act provides clear protections for Iowa families by ensuring medical-cardholder status cannot be used as grounds for disqualification in parental or foster eligibility decisions.

  • Patient Rights Clause
  • Newborn and Infant Exposure Clause
  • Perinatal and Postnatal Care Clause
