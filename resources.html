Patient Rights (Overview)

  • If you are a lawful patient under Iowa Code § 124E, status alone is not neglect or unfitness.
  • Child-welfare decisions must hinge on impairment, not metabolites.
  • Keep documentation: registry card, physician certification, dosing guidance.
