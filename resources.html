Patient Rights (Overview)

Plain-language summary — not legal advice.

  • If you are a lawful patient under Iowa’s medical-cannabis program (Iowa Code § 124E), your status alone should not be treated as neglect or unfitness.
  • Child-welfare safety decisions must focus on impairment (behavior, ability to provide care), not metabolite tests alone.
  • Keep documentation handy: patient registration/card, physician certification, dosing guidance, and current medications.
  • If questioned, ask what impairment indicators are being observed (attention, coordination, judgment), and request these be documented.
  • You may request that any toxicology results be interpreted with detection windows and functional evidence in mind.
